Rules:

1. Player 1 starts the game.

2. Each player rolls the dice (click "roll dice" button) until they either roll a 1 or click the "hold" button

3. If you roll a 1, your current score resets to 0 and you lose your turn

4. If you click the hold button, your current score is saved (under the player name) and you end your turn

5. The first player to reach 100 wins the game! Click start to begin
